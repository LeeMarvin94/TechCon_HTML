History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small meetup of local developers. Over the years, it has grown into a global conference, hosting industry leaders, researchers, and innovators. Each year we aim to push the boundaries of what technology can do to improve lives.

Mission

Our mission is to foster innovation, education, and community among technologists. We strive to create a platform where ideas are shared, collaborations are formed, and future tech leaders are inspired.
